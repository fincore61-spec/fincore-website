Common Questions

What types of businesses does FinCore serve?
+
We serve startups, SMEs, manufacturers, retailers, e-commerce businesses, healthcare providers, and professional service firms across India. Our team has sector-specific expertise to handle the unique financial needs of each industry.
How quickly can you onboard my business?
+
Our streamlined onboarding process takes 24-48 hours. After your initial consultation, we'll have your accounts migrated, workflows set up, and your dedicated account manager introduced — all within two business days.
Do you work with cloud accounting software?
+
Yes. We're proficient in Tally, QuickBooks, Zoho Books, FreshBooks, Sage, and all major accounting platforms. We can work with your existing software or recommend the best fit for your business.
What's included in your Virtual CFO service?
+
Our Virtual CFO service includes MIS reporting, cash flow forecasting, budgeting, board/investor presentations, fundraising support, financial strategy advisory, and a dedicated senior finance professional available on-demand.
How do you ensure data security and confidentiality?
+
We follow bank-grade security protocols. All client data is encrypted, stored on secure servers with limited access, and covered by strict NDAs. Our team undergoes regular security training, and we comply with all data protection regulations.
What is your pricing structure?
+
We offer flexible, transparent pricing based on your business size, transaction volume, and service requirements. We have monthly retainer packages starting from ₹4,999/month for startups, with custom pricing for larger enterprises. No hidden fees.
